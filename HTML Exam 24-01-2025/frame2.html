Welcome to Don Bosco Tech

Don Bosco Tech Society offers market-driven, employment-linked vocational training to the economically and socially deprived youth (NEET). We are recognized by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) as a Skill Training Provider. Your donation will directly fund the skill development of these youth ensuring them a secure future. This isn't just a gift for today; it's an investment in their lifelong success and in nation building. Registered under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 (Reg. No. S/55112) of 2006. Your Donation is eligible for tax deduction under section of 80G of Income Tax..
